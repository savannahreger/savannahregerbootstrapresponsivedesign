Who We Are
What we do
Welcome to Madison 101! This is a student produced magazine that paints the picture of what it's like to be a student at JMU. In the magazine, our staff writers dive in to different aspects of being a student, from diet culture to football games, learn what its like to be a student here at James Madison University.
We are a student run magazine desgined for incoming students. We look at what JMU is like everyday and what students can do on and off campus.
